What to Do While Waiting for Emergency Service

When you've Called for Emergency service, there are steps you Can take to ensure safety and potentially minimize damage:

For Gas Leaks or ElectriCal Issues

  • Turn off the main gas or electriCal supply if safe to do so
  • Evacuate the area if you smell gas
  • Avoid using electriCal switches or creating sparks
  • Open windows for ventilation
  • Wait outside until Emergency technicians arrive

For System Failures

  • Check circuit breakers and reset if necessary
  • Ensure thermostat is set properly
  • Replace air filters if obviously dirty
  • Clear any obvious obstructions from outdoor units
  • Turn off system if unusual noises or odors are present

Common Mini Split Problems We Repair

Our expert technicians diagnose and repair all types of mini split issues, from simple maintenance problems to complex system failures. We service all major brands including Mitsubishi, Daikin, Fujitsu, LG, and more. Common problems we address include:

Cooling and Heating Issues

  • System not cooling or heating effectively
  • Inconsistent temperature control
  • Hot or cold spots in treated areas
  • System cycling on and off frequently
  • Poor airflow from indoor units
  • Unusual noise during operation

ElectriCal and Component Problems

  • System won't turn on or respond to remote
  • Compressor failure or malfunction
  • Fan motor problems
  • Control board failures
  • Sensor and thermostat issues
  • ElectriCal connection problems

Refrigerant and mechanical Issues

  • Refrigerant leaks and low refrigerant levels
  • Frozen evaporator coils
  • Dirty or clogged filters and coils
  • Condensate drain clogs and water leaks
  • Outdoor unit problems
  • Line set and connection issues
